The greatest Bengali who ever lived Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman lies lifeless on the stairs of his Dhanmondi 32 residence. His body riddled with bullets. He was assassinated by a handful of army renegades as part of a larger national and international political conspiracy hatched by anti-liberation forces in the pre-dawn hours of August 15. It is the darkest day in the history of Bangladesh (August 15, 1975).
